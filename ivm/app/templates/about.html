Key Features

  • Real-Time Tracking: Monitor inventory levels in real time, ensuring you are always aware of stock availability.
  • Product Categorization: Seamlessly organize products by categories, making it easier to manage and locate items.

  • Data Analytics: Gain insights into sales trends, high-demand products, and inventory turnover for data-driven decisions.
  • Automation: Automate stock updates and notifications for low inventory levels, ensuring customer demands are always met.
